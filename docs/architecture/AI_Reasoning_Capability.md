# AI + NLP + ML Reasoning Capability — Engineering Intelligence System

## Vision
The goal is to build an **Engineering Intelligence System** capable of reasoning about real-world mechanical and structural behavior.
This system combines LLMs, Engineering NLP, Geometry Intelligence, Physics, and ML Prediction.

---

## 1. Core AI Architecture
- **NLP Layer:** Intent + Entity extraction (Loads, Materials).
- **Reasoning Layer:** Geometry + Physics + Rules.
- **ML Layer:** Risk prediction based on historical/simulated data.
- **Synthesis:** AI Engineering Explanation Generator.

---

## 2. NLP Layer for Engineering
- **Intent Classifier:** Detects goals (Failure prediction, Optimization, Stiffness).
- **Entity Extractor:** Identifies variables (20kg load, Aluminum material).

---

## 3. Hybrid AI Reasoning Architecture (CRITICAL)
**LLM + Physics + Geometry + Rules + ML + Simulation.**
- Pure LLMs hallucinate engineering precision.
- Hybrid systems stay physically grounded and explainable.

---

## 4. ML Risk Prediction Layer
- **Goal:** Predict failure probability based on geometry ratios and material properties.
- **Recommended Models:** XGBoost, Random Forest (Tree models excel at structured engineering data).

---

## 5. Development Strategy
- **Phase 1:** Heuristics and symbolic reasoning.
- **Phase 2:** Engineering embeddings and RAG-based knowledge retrieval.
- **Phase 3:** Multi-agent coordination (Geometry Agent, Physics Agent, etc.).
- **Phase 4:** Engineering Intelligence Flywheel (Self-learning from simulations).
